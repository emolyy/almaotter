About Us

We are students studying Computer Science at University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign. Thank you so much for playing our game! Welcome to our computer game, developed by Computer Science students at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign!

In a world where environmental sustainability is more crucial than ever, we aim to promote awareness and action through immersive gaming experiences. Our game encourages players to adopt environmentally friendly practices, showcasing how a little extra effort today can lead to a brighter and more sustainable future tomorrow. Join us on this journey towards a greener tomorrow, where every small action counts towards a more sustainable and thriving world for generations to come.
